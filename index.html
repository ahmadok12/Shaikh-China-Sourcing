What we do

One partner for everything between the factory and your door.

Whether you have a product idea or a packed shipping schedule, we cover the full sourcing journey — transparently, and on the ground in China.

01

Product Sourcing

Tell us what you need. We find the right factory at the right price, negotiate on your behalf, and send you a clear quote — no markup games.

02

Factory Audit & Verification

On-site visits, supplier vetting and compliance checks before you commit a single dollar — so you only work with factories that are real and reliable.

03

Quality Control & Inspection

Sample approval and pre-shipment inspections with photo & video reports, so the goods that ship are exactly the goods you ordered.

04

Shipping & Freight

Door-to-door logistics by sea, air and express. We consolidate cargo, handle customs and track every shipment until it lands with you.

Custom branding, OEM/ODM, logo printing and retail-ready packaging that makes a Chinese-made product feel unmistakably yours.
